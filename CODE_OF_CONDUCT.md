# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Liquid C2 is committed to providing a professional, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone participating in our projects and community.

---

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and professional
- Communicate constructively
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on technical merit
- Respect differing viewpoints

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes, but is not limited to:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Abusive or offensive language
- Personal attacks
- Publishing private or sensitive information
- Disruptive or unprofessional conduct

---

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take appropriate action, including:

- Warning the contributor
- Removing content
- Temporarily or permanently banning participation

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- GitHub repositories
- Issues and pull requests
- Discussions and comments
- Any official Liquid C2 community spaces

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it via:

📧 support@liquid.tech

All reports will be reviewed confidentially and fairly.
